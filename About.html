About Bree Reads

Bree Reads is a book blog for people who are looking for books to read and reviews to help them determine whether or not they will enjoy the book before fully committing to purchase it.

Bree Reads is a blog for people that have an interest in books and want to gather a second or third opinion before completely making the decision to purchase the book. Bree Reads is not a blog that will consist of spoilers of the books that were read.

We are looking to create a judgement free atmosphere for people who have anxiety and is looking for an alternative way of finding reviews for books they may have an interest in. This blog page will consist of books of different genres one is interested in and will allow them to discover other books they may be interested in.
